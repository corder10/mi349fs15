My Black Cat

Iverson is his name, jumping around, wilding out is his game, but he wasn't always so big and healthy. It was a cold late September, when a mother of two heard a faint meow coming from near the engine of her car. For warmth, cats will often find their way to a car engine. That cat was very malnourished and for being over 2 months old was very small. After taking him in and giving him a warm place to stay, the mother of two found a place for this very sickly kitten with me.

The kitten would purr non-stop just at the sight of humans. Knowing he was outside for a while, we took him to a vet who learned he had worms and fleas. After proper treatment and a healthy diet, that cat grew quickly! Iverson is that cat, and he's the cat you see before you today!
